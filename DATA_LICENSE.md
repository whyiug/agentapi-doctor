# Data License Policy

## No public dataset yet

AgentAPI Doctor does not currently publish a Registry dataset or compatibility
observation export. This document defines the intended license boundaries so a
future service cannot treat every uploaded byte as public-domain data.

## Repository content

Source code, schemas, packs, examples, and documentation in this repository are
licensed under the [Apache License 2.0](LICENSE) unless a file carries a
different notice. Fixtures must also retain their provenance, SPDX identifier,
author information where required, and allowed-use record.

## Future public fact projection

If a hosted Registry is approved and launched, a submitter may explicitly
dedicate an enumerated, non-sensitive factual projection of an observation
under [CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).
The exact projected fields, object digest, and affirmative consent must be
shown before publication.

CC0 would apply only to that projection. It would not automatically apply to:

- raw or normalized artifacts;
- requests, responses, prompts, model text, or logs;
- identities, signatures, comments, or owner responses;
- source code, packs, schemas, or documentation;
- third-party trademarks or copyrighted material; or
- facts the submitter lacks authority to dedicate.

The absence of a rights statement is not consent. Stars, forks, issue
participation, public endpoint availability, or silence do not authorize
inclusion.

## Underlying artifacts

Submitters retain their rights in underlying artifacts. Any future Registry
needs separately effective terms granting only the non-exclusive rights needed
to receive, store, validate, redact, derive the approved projection, display,
and administer those artifacts. The current
[REGISTRY_TERMS.md](REGISTRY_TERMS.md) is a draft and grants no such operational
permission today.

Questions about a particular source or fixture must be resolved before it is
merged or published. Content with unclear rights remains private or excluded.
