# Hosted Service Acceptable Use — Draft, Not in Effect

> **DRAFT ONLY.** AgentAPI Doctor does not operate a hosted Registry or public
> runner today. This document is a proposed safety baseline for a future
> service, not an active service agreement.

## Intended boundary

AgentAPI Doctor is for authorized compatibility research, local validation,
reproducible diagnostics, and responsible upstream remediation. It is not a
public-target scanner, exploitation platform, traffic laundering service, or
compatibility-certification marketplace.

An effective future policy must prohibit using project-operated services to:

- test a target without authorization;
- scan, probe, fuzz, exploit, overload, or evade controls on public or
  third-party systems;
- submit malware, weaponized payloads, credential theft, persistence, or data
  exfiltration workflows;
- upload production secrets, private keys, authentication tokens, personal
  data, confidential logs, or material without sufficient rights;
- access cloud metadata or internal network resources;
- manipulate trust labels, observations, freshness, rankings, disputes, or
  ownership claims;
- impersonate a vendor, maintainer, reviewer, or project owner;
- infringe copyright, trademark, privacy, contract, or other rights;
- harass users or publish sensitive vulnerability details prematurely; or
- bypass quotas, isolation, quarantine, retention, or deletion controls.

Good-faith security research remains welcome only against local fixtures,
project-owned test infrastructure, or systems for which the researcher has
explicit authorization. The repository's boundaries are described in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Future enforcement requirements

Before launch, the project must define proportionate suspension, evidence
preservation, notice, appeal, emergency action, conflict-of-interest, and
law-enforcement-request procedures. Sponsors and paying users may not purchase
exceptions, higher compatibility labels, or deletion of valid failures.

No enforcement action or user acceptance is implied while this document
remains a draft and no hosted service exists.
