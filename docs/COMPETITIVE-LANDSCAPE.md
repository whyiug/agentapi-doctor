# Competitive Landscape Research Protocol

**Status:** no completed comparison or external outreach evidence is recorded
in this document.

P00 research compares named projects only from current primary sources and
across the same dimensions: capability overlap, architecture, evidence model,
client-observed behavior, governance, reuse/collaboration opportunity, and the
case for or against a separate project.

The final matrix must record source URL, actual retrieval time, commit/release,
content digest, license/reuse status, and uncertainty. It must identify where a
competitor is the better choice and must not infer endorsement or willingness
from silence.

Outreach drafts are human-send-only. No Issue, email, review, interview,
adoption, or external willingness is claimed until a real party responds and
the allowed non-sensitive evidence is imported through the protected process.
