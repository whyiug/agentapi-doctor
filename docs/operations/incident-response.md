# Incident Response

Potential incident classes include secret exposure, malicious pack/driver or
artifact, signer/reviewer compromise, forged observation, Registry abuse,
privacy withdrawal failure, availability loss, and supply-chain compromise.

## Minimum process

1. Triage privately and identify the affected subjects/digests.
2. Contain only project-owned resources; never stop unrelated processes or
   systems.
3. Preserve sanitized audit evidence and restrict sensitive copies.
4. Revoke or quarantine affected identities, artifacts, or promotion channels
   without deleting history needed for investigation.
5. Remediate with tests, migration, and re-verification.
6. Notify affected users under the effective policy and law.
7. Publish a bounded post-incident review after embargoed details are safe.

The public [security policy](../../SECURITY.md) is the current reporting entry.
The hosted escalation roster, contacts, and exercise record remain unset
because no service or Security Team exists.
