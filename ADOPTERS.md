An entry may be added only after the project or its authorized representative:

1. confirms that it uses AgentAPI Doctor in the stated way;
2. agrees to be named publicly;
3. identifies a verifiable repository, deployment description, or contact;
4. approves the wording and material limitations; and
5. permits removal or correction if the statement becomes stale.

Stars, forks, clones, downloads, issue comments, experiments, design targets,
or silence are not adoption evidence. A local test performed by this project's
authors does not make the tested project an adopter.

Future entries should state the integration surface, first verified date, most
recent verification date, and supporting public evidence without publishing
secrets or private operational data.
