# Hosted Registry Terms — Draft, Not in Effect

> **DRAFT ONLY.** AgentAPI Doctor does not operate a hosted Registry, upload
> service, public runner, or compatibility Matrix today. These terms have not
> been legally reviewed, accepted by users, or put into effect. They are an
> engineering checklist, not a contract or legal advice.

## Purpose of this draft

Any future hosted service needs terms that distinguish public compatibility
facts from private artifacts and provide enforceable rules for authorization,
validation, disputes, withdrawal, and abuse. Those terms must be reviewed for
the jurisdictions in which the service and its users operate before launch.

## Items an effective version must define

An effective version must, at minimum, identify:

- the service operator and a real contact method;
- eligibility and the authority required to test or submit a target;
- the exact service, storage locations, subprocessors, and jurisdictions;
- the accepted data classes and prohibited sensitive data;
- the license for the public fact projection under
  [DATA_LICENSE.md](DATA_LICENSE.md);
- the limited license needed to host, copy, validate, redact, and derive from
  underlying artifacts;
- ownership challenge, attestation, signature, and audit requirements;
- moderation, quarantine, dispute, owner response, supersede, tombstone,
  withdrawal, and physical-deletion procedures;
- retention, backup, and restoration treatment;
- acceptable-use enforcement and appeal;
- service availability, suspension, termination, warranty, liability, and
  governing-law terms; and
- the change-notice and affirmative-acceptance process.

## Non-negotiable engineering boundaries

Future terms must not authorize the project to:

- sell uploaded traces or user behavior;
- publish raw secrets or private payloads;
- turn payment or sponsorship into a passing result, higher trust label, or
  removal of a valid failure;
- silently expand a user's private artifact into CC0 material; or
- represent a project-generated observation as official vendor certification.

The service must show the exact public projection before submission and bind
the accepted terms, projection, source identity, and object digests in a durable
record.

## No acceptance today

Reading, cloning, or contributing to this source repository does not accept
hosted-service terms. Source contributions are governed by
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) and the repository license. Registry terms
will become effective only if a real service launches with a dated version,
operator identity, reviewed privacy notice, and explicit acceptance flow.
